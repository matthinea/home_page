App.ly

Site | Repo

App.ly is a Rails app for prospective college students and their families. Search 500 colleges and universities, browse useful school data, bookmark schools you're interested in, review schools, and track your applications. I added school reviews, configured the Google oauth sign-in, and added automatic email confirmation to the app.

Seattle Crime Data Portal

Site | Repo

Built to simplify interaction with Seattle's OpenData API. Crime statistics for searched-for time periods and areas are displayed to the screen with other relevant data. Form validations use the database to check whether beats belong to precincts, otherwise the data comes from real-time hits to the API.
