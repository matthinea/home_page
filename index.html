Web development. Algorithmic composition. Data, data, data.

Nowadays I develop web applications full-time to stay out of trouble. I use Ruby on Rails, because Ruby is beautiful. I also program synths in SuperCollider, because 8 hours a day of coding just wasn't enough. Find me at any of the links below, or drop me a line via your favorite email client.
